Background:

The data set consists of a sample of n = 500 school children. A research team was interested in measuring the average height of children leaving in an urban area and children leaving in a rural area. In the dataset you will find the following variables:

  • ethnicity: childrenâ€™s ethnicity (1: White, 2: Black, 3: Asian, 4: Other)
  • area: classification of the area the children live in (1: Rural and 0: Urban)
  • Height2014: childrenâ€™s height as measured in 2014.
  • Height2018: childrenâ€™s height as measured in 2018.
  • nutrition2014: a classification of the quality of childrenâ€™s nutrition by the research team (based on appropriate parameters, interviews and observation) made in 2014 (1: bad, 2: average, 3: good).
  • nutrition2018: a classification of the quality of childrenâ€™s nutrition by the research team (based on appropriate parameters, interviews and observation) made in 2018 (1: bad, 2: average, 3: good).

Task 3

The researcher hypothesised that the studentsâ€™ height in 2014 was 150 cm. Use the appropriate commands to test the researcherâ€™s hypothesis. Write the null and the alternative hypotheses and report on the results.

The mean height of the students in 2014 was 146.0 cm. This was significantly lower than 150cm (t=-40.402, df=499, p<0.001; 95% CI: 145.7773, 146.1689). Therefore, we reject the null hypothesis and infer that the students in 2014 were shorter than 150cm.
